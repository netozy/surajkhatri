Types Of Organizational Goal

1) Misson Goals

Misson goals are the last destination of organization. They are considered as philosophy or slogan of organization. Misson goals are set by top level management to explain about reasons of the existence of organization. There may be different mission on the basic of the nature of the organization . For example: to be a world class university is the mission of university. Quality product is the mission of the organization. Connecting people may be mission of communication company.

2) Strategic Goals

Strategic goals are long term goals of an organization set by top level management with concerading mission of organization. While setting strategic goal there should be shout analyist ( strength, threath, wickness, and opportanities). Proper strategic goal provide proper guidelines and direction where organization want to reach strategic goal also provide proper technical to achieve the mission.

3) Tatical Goals

With conserading strategic goal different department set the tatical goals. They are related to the implementation of strategic goal. For example; quality product may be a tatical goal pf production department, complete an errors less, accounting may be the tatical goal of finance, increase in sells volume is the goals of sells department, right person to the right position at the righttime is the tatical goals of human resources department.

4) Operationals Goals

Operational goals are set by lower level management like coordinater, incharge, unite manager etc. all operational goal are related to the day to day operation of organization. Allocation of resources, repair and mantenance, reporting etc, are some example of operational goal.

Features Of Organization Goals

Organizational goals are the guideline that show the last destination or target but just setting goal is not a complete solution of organization that must be effectuive. A effectuive organizational goal should have the following features;

1) Specific Goal

Organization goal are Specific and clear organization can get clear and guideline destination fulfill the target vague and unclear types of goal should be avoided hospital should set the goal as providing W.H.O standard health service, establishment of I.S.O 9001:2000 standard organization, providing quality Harvard university eduction, etc are some example of specific of goals of the different organization.

2) Measurable Goal

While setting goal organization should measure that goal in term of quality, quantity, price, etc. For example: production a fewa noddles with nepal standarrd per packet Rs.20 of 75 grams. If organization goalsare not Measurable it can't meet the target with proper standard.

3) Acceptable Goal

An effective goal should be acceptable by stake holdders like employees, society, customer, government, etc. If there is no direct support of concern person and party organization can't fulfill that goal in future. Therefore before setting the goal an organization needs to collect the opinion and aggrement all stakeholders.

4) Realistic

Realistic is the major quality or features of organizational goal it shouldn't be imanagetive and over-ambititious while setting the goal an organization needs to consideration availiable of resources, human resources, finance and other physical resources as well as competoors and environmental factor.

5) Time Fame

Organization set the long-term, mid-term and short-term goal with the specific target. Certain goals must be achieve with in one year, five year, ten year, twenty year, etc. If there is no time bound there may not be proper target, program, budget and control machanism.

Goal Formation Process

Organization should formulate the SMART goal(Specific, Measurable, Acceptable, Realistic and Time Bound) for the formulation of effective goal the following step to be followed:

1) Environmental Scanning

Environmental scanning initial stage goal formulation process for the formulation effective goal top level management needs to study the internal and external environment. Internal environment provides the information strength and weakness of organization similarly oppurtunities and threats are the outcome of external environment(PEST) political, economical, social & cultural and technology environment. After getting detail information about organization can set the vision, mission and strategic.

2) Setting Overall Goal

After the study of environment and SWOT analystic organization should set the overall goal like: earning profit for business organization, providing international standard service is the goal for service motive organization, quality education is the goal of college universities. Similarly an organization can determine the mission and strategic goal for the future to get clear guideline and launch the different program.

3) Setting Specific Goal

After the formulation of overall goal of an organization different department or level should formulate specific goal like, quality control is the specific goal of production department effective distrubtion of product is the specific goal of marketing department. Right person to the right position at the right time is the specific goal of human resource complete and transperent. Accounting is the specific goal of finance department. With considering specific goal of particular department individual employees also can set the specific goal as well as operational goal for example, proper allowacation of resources, maintaining proper condition of machinery equipment reporting in time, etc are some example of specific goal of individual employees.
