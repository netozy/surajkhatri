Fundamentals Of Organizing

Concept Of Organizing

Organizing is the important function of management. Organizing is the process of defining and grouping the activities of enterprise and establishing the authority relationship among them. In simple word, It is the process of breaking down the activities into smalllest possible components to take of labour division and specialization. It involves deciding the ways and means with which the plans can be implemented.
