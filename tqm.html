Total Quality Management (TQM)

Total quality management is a way of managing to improve the effectivenes, flexibility and competitives of a business as a whole. TQM means every member in organization is responsible for the quality of the products or services. TQM is setting quality goals, implementing quality systems and processes, measuring and monitoring quality. It also focus on customer needs and expectations and the involvement of employees in the quality improvement process. TQM help organizations achieve a number of benefits like increased customer satisfaction, improved product, increased productivity, reduced costs and motivation. TQM help organizations to achieve the demands of a rapidly changing business environment and to stay competitive in the marketplace.

Obejectives

1) Better, less veriable quality of the of the product and service.

2) Quickness, less variable response in process to consumer needs.

3) Greater flexibility in adjusting to consumer's shifting requirement.

4) Lower cost through quality improvement and elimination of non-value adding work.

    • What Is Deming Management ?

    Deming management is known as the Deming cycle or PDCA cycle is a management method that was developed by W. Edwards Deming is an American statistician, professor and consultant. The Deming management is a continuous improvement model that focuses on: Plan, Do, Check, and Act. According to Deming, management is the creation and continious inprovement of the organizational system. Creative and improve management system leads to increase in value for customers of the product or service. The Deming management is a continuous process and it is essential to repeat the management to achieve continuous improvement. It emphasizes the importance of data-driven decision-making, teamwork and continuous improvement. This management method has been widely used in many industries, manufacturing, healthcare and education. Deming principles have been used to develop Total Quality Management (TQM), Lean Manufacturing, Training, speed, Statistical quality control and Six Sigma. Deming management has helped organizations to improve quality, increase efficiency and reduce costs.
