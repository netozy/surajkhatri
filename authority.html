Delegation Of Authority

Delegation of authority is the process of sharing the task with it's corresponding authority with the subordinates. Single/individual power (physical/mental) is limiteed. Degree of delegation of authority is depend on manager's degree of management capabilities. It's also downward authority transfer. It's also known as division of work. It has advantage on quick decision and speedy Performances. Delegation of authority can also improve efficiency by making more employees accountable for their own work and activities. Less time and energy is spent on monitoring and managing employees who are capable and competent. Team becomes more capable and able to achieve higher performance as a result. The purpose of delegation of authority is productive and well-functioning workplace. Delegation of authority requires clearly defined expectations, progress monitoring and evaluation of the results.
