Introduction To Management

Management is the proper utilization of resources that is human resources, finance, physical resources, information, method, etc by application of planning, organizing, directing, controlling, cooordinating, etc to achieve the mission strategic and objective of organization in effective and efficient manner. Management is the comprehension term it can't define at the exact meaning but it has great meaning and important for the proper management utilization of available resources for the smooth resources accomplishment for short, mid and long term goal. It is intangible activities that regulates the entire or whole organization.

Characteristics Of Management

  • Utilization of resources
  • Goal orientation
  • Group activities
  • Creative action
  • Universal process
  • Dynamic function
  • Continious process
  • Both tangible and intangible

    • Functions Of Management

    The sucess or failure of any organization depends upon the its own management system. Effective management is the major componenet for the smooth operation and success of organization. Therefore management has to perform the following functions:

    Planning Function

    Each and every organization should have the clear and effective plan because it provides proper guidlines about what types of activities to be performs in future. Specially planning functions indicates what product or service to be produce? when and how to produce? what types of resources are required? etc. Planning function of management includes the following activities to decide in advance in future.
    Seting goal
    Selection of best cources off action
    Forcasting work schedule
    Identification required resources to be manage for the implementation of plan

    Organizing Function

    Organizing is the fundatation of management and effective organizing facilated for the implementation of plan. Organizing clearly defines structure of organization as top level, middle and lower level. Similarly it defines the different departments on the basic nature of activitie, delegation of authority and responsibility maintaining proper coordination among the department to obtain joint effort for the achievement of overall goal. Organizing function includes the following activities:
    Defining organizational structure
    Grouping of activities
    Development of different department on the basic of the nature obsurve
    Difining authority and responsibility
    Maintaining the coordination among the employees or working groups

    Staffing Function

    At present employees are considered as human capital or valuable assets of organization skill experence and well trained employees make an organization success. Therefore management should perform the staffing function for the selection of right candidates at the right post, at the time in the right number in similar manner. Organization should motivate the employees by the application of reward, fair promotion system, etc. Management needs to perform the following activities regarding staffing functions:
    Recruitment for right candidates
    Selection of right candidates for the right post
    Development of employees through taining, workshop, orientation, etc.
    Placement and assign the job
    Evaluation of employees
    Maintenanace of employees

    Directing Function

    Proper direction of manager or exucative chief provides the proper guidlines write job at the right time. Directing function includes guiding, monitoring, inspiring, motivating, communicating and maintaining coordination among the department working groups, etc an effective directing function includes the following activities:
    Supervising the performances of supervior and inspiring them
    Leading and incourageing followers towards the mission and goals of organization
    To motivates the employees towards their job by the application of rewards, fair promotion, incouragement and participation
    Maintaining good coordination among the different department, working group as well as different functions
    Management perform the communicating function information about plan and policy decision from top to lower level, similar reports suggestion canbe collected from lower level to top level.
