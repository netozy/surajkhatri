What is EBITA?

EBITA (Earnings before interest, taxes, and amortization ) is measure of company profitibility.It help to compare one company with another especially for investor to invest in that company. It provide the core value of company. It show how much dividend paid and also show how company is operating. EBITA help to show the performance of the company.While investing investor use to measure the health of company. While calculating Net income,interest,taxes,and amortization is needed.
